P

Pensford Cap Pricer

LoanBoss API for Excel

API Key
Formula
=PENSFORD.CAP(notional, strike, effective, termination, index, output, headers, direction)
Parameters
ParamDescription
notionalLoan amount (e.g. 25000000)
strikeRate ceiling — 4.5 for 4.50%
effectiveStart date. Blank = today. e.g. "2026-06-01"
terminationEnd date or months. e.g. 36 or "2029-06-01"
indexRate index. "TSFR1M" (default) or "NYFED"
outputField(s) to return. Blank = all. See below.
headersTRUE (default) or FALSE
direction"H" horizontal (default) or "V" vertical
Output Keys
KeyReturns
bankAdjBank spread / fees
capCostTotal cap premium
mtmMark-to-market (ex fees)
mtmPercentMTM as % of notional
midMid-market price (= capCost)
netBenefitNet benefit to borrower
pvPV of total projected payouts
Examples

Basic — $25M, 4.50% strike, 3-year:

=PENSFORD.CAP(25000000, 4.5, , 36)

Three fields with headers, horizontal:

=PENSFORD.CAP(25000000, 4.5, , 36, , "capCost,netBenefit,pv", TRUE, "H")

Specific dates, vertical output:

=PENSFORD.CAP(25000000, 4.5, "2026-06-01", "2029-06-01", , "capCost,bankAdj", TRUE, "V")

Cell references:

=PENSFORD.CAP(A2, B2, C2, D2)
